# Emoji

CleverKeys includes a full emoji keyboard with categories, search, and recent emoji history.

## Quick Summary

| What | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Purpose** | Insert emoji and emoticons |
| **Access** | Emoji key or long-press Enter |
| **Features** | Categories, recents, skin tones |

## Accessing Emoji

### Method 1: Emoji Key

If your layout has an emoji key (smiley face icon):
1. Tap the **emoji key** on the bottom row
2. Browse or search emoji
3. Tap to insert

### Method 2: Long-Press Enter

1. **Long-press** the Enter key
2. Select the emoji option from popup
3. Emoji keyboard opens

### Method 3: Long-Press Comma

On some layouts:
1. **Long-press** the comma key
2. Select emoji from the popup menu

## Emoji Keyboard Layout

The emoji keyboard is organized into categories:

| Category | Icon | Contents |
|----------|------|----------|
| **Recent** | Clock | Recently used emoji |
| **Smileys** | Smile | Faces and emotions |
| **People** | Person | People and gestures |
| **Animals** | Paw | Animals and nature |
| **Food** | Apple | Food and drink |
| **Activities** | Ball | Sports and activities |
| **Travel** | Car | Places and transportation |
| **Objects** | Lightbulb | Objects and tools |
| **Symbols** | Heart | Symbols and signs |
| **Flags** | Flag | Country flags |

## Using Emoji

### Browsing

1. Open emoji keyboard
2. Tap category icons at bottom to navigate
3. Scroll within each category
4. Tap any emoji to insert

### Recent Emoji

Your most recently used emoji appear in the first category for quick access.

### Skin Tone Variations

For emoji with skin tone options:
1. **Long-press** the emoji
2. Select your preferred skin tone
3. This becomes the default for that emoji

## Tips and Tricks

- **Recents**: Check recents first for frequently used emoji
- **Scroll**: Swipe left/right to see more in each category
- **Return to letters**: Tap ABC or the keyboard icon
- **Multiple emoji**: Stay on emoji keyboard to insert several

> [!TIP]
> The Recent category learns your habits - frequently used emoji rise to the top.

## Common Questions

### Q: Why do emoji look different in some apps?
A: Emoji appearance depends on the app and Android version. CleverKeys sends the Unicode character; the app decides how to display it.

### Q: How do I search for emoji?
A: Some layouts include a search bar at the top of the emoji keyboard. Type a word like "happy" to find matching emoji.

### Q: Can I add custom emoji?
A: CleverKeys uses standard Unicode emoji. Custom emoji/stickers depend on the app you're typing in.
