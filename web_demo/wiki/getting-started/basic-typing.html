Basic Typing

Learn the fundamentals of typing with CleverKeys, from simple taps to useful shortcuts.

Quick Summary

WhatDescription
PurposeType text using tap and basic gestures
DifficultyBeginner
Time to learn5 minutes

Tap Typing

Tap individual keys to type letters:

  • 1. Tap a letter - Types that letter
  • 2. Tap space - Inserts a space
  • 3. Tap backspace - Deletes previous character
  • 4. Tap enter - New line or submit (depends on app)

Shift and Caps Lock

  • • Tap shift once - Next letter is uppercase
  • • Double-tap shift - Caps lock (all uppercase)
  • • Tap shift again - Return to lowercase

Numbers and Symbols

  • • Tap ?123 - Switch to numbers/symbols
  • • Tap ABC - Return to letters
  • • Long-press a key - See additional characters

Using Predictions

As you type, predictions appear above the keyboard:

  • 1. Center prediction - Best match (tap to insert)
  • 2. Side predictions - Alternative suggestions
  • 3. Tap a prediction - Replaces current word

Tip

Tap the center prediction or press space to accept it.

Useful Shortcuts

ShortcutAction
Double-tap spaceInsert period and space
Long-press periodMore punctuation options
Long-press commaOpen settings
Swipe backspace leftDelete entire word

Basic Punctuation

Access common punctuation:

  • • Period: Bottom row, next to space
  • • Comma: Bottom row, left of space
  • • Question mark: Short swipe up on period
  • • Exclamation: Short swipe up on comma

Moving the Cursor

  • • Tap in text - Move cursor to that position
  • • Slide on spacebar - Move cursor left/right
  • • Arrow keys - If enabled in settings

Tips for Beginners

  • • Don't rush: Accuracy improves with practice
  • • Use predictions: They save keystrokes
  • • Try swipe typing: Often faster than tapping (see Swipe Typing)
  • • Explore settings: Customize to your preference

Common Questions

Q: How do I undo autocorrect?

A: Tap the original word in predictions, or tap backspace and retype.

Q: How do I type accented characters?

A: Long-press the base letter (e.g., long-press 'e' for é, è, ê).

Q: How do I access emoji?

A: Tap the emoji key (smiley face) or long-press Enter.
